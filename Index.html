An Ode to Empty Notebooks

In between the bindings of every empty notebook lives a sweet snippet of comfort.
Paused, poised and balancing, in the stoic arms of plastic and cardboard is, like the
bottomless, unexplored ocean, or the great expanses of enormous outer space, a realm of
possibilities.
